About This Project

Hello! I am Mohith Choppara, a final-year B.Tech student in the stream of Internet of Things (IoT) at SRGEC.

This project, Campus Bites, is aimed at simplifying food ordering in our college canteen. With this website, students can easily browse the cafeteria menu and place their food orders using their mobile devices, saving time and avoiding queues.

The primary goal of this project is to bring convenience to students while also helping the cafeteria manage orders efficiently.
